Privacy Policy

This document describes the privacy policy for the Weekend Flyer App.

This app will contact certain U.S. government websites (those listed under the settings option) to retrieve weater data as well as selected airport information. Upon request it will also open a webpage to the company website for support.

With the exception of these web transactions, no other information should be shared between the users device or these other entitites.
